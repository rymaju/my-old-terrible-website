Professional Work

APEx Health and Wellness Center

an interactive architectual rendering of a new school facility

Summary

For 5 months, I worked with my school to create a conceptual architectural rendering of a new school health and wellness facility called APEx. This architectural rendering was my first paid job. This project was the longest and most stressful that I’ve ever done, but I’m glad I was able to use my Unity3D and Blender knowledge to give back to my school community. I encountered a lot of challenges during this process, if you want to see some of the details of how I built this take a look at my detailed postmortem here. Additionally, if you want to download this rendering to see it in better quality, go to my itch.io page for this project.
