Kevin

my first real attempt at a Unity game

Summary

Kevin was the first real Unity3D project I ever made. I had finished Unity3D tutorials and made a small game before, but this was the first team project I worked on. For about 6 months we spent half an hour every week to work on the project. It was a gruesome development process and we had to scrap a lot of content because we didnâ€™t like the design. I think that this experience shaped my attitudes as a designer, and showed me the importance of proper planning and design. Simply making pixel art and mindlessly writing code does not make a good game. You need deadlines and a general design plan in order to minimize wasted effort and increase the quality of the game.
