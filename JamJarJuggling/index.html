Game Jam Submission

Jam Jar Juggling

a game about juggling jars of jam

Postmortem

Summary

Jam Jar Juggling was my first real game jam, I got second place I worked in a team. From design to conception it was a struggle but it was rewarding. I think that for a first entry in a game jam, it was very impressive. I think it was due to an intersting core mechanic and a charming aesthetic.

What Went Right

Team communication was excellent at the beginning. My team and I spent 4 hours in a skype call drawing out possible ideas. The division of labor was decent. I was the dedicated programmer and designer and my two teammates helped with art and planning. The core mechanic only came up on the second day of development. We found that bouncing the jars repeatedly was much more interesting than simply moving them. With that, we changed almost every part of the game to focus on this mechanic. The game improved dramatically with that one decision.

What Went Wrong

The entire production process was a nightmare. My team and I were very unfocused and we barely finished in time. We painted everything in gimp and threw out almost half of all the “art” that we produced. I failed to give strong leadership and that affected the quality of the final product.
