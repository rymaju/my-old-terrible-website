Mentored Project

Tendr

a low quality take on the bird dating sim genre

Summary

Tendr was a very special game that I has a hand in creating. However, unlike my previous works, my contribution to this project was simply creative direction and production. It was essentially a way to teach members of the club how to participate in a team game jam. I worked as the creative director, trying to make sure that everything fit the aesthetic and feel of the game design document that we had created for this project. Additionally, as the producer I tried to keep everyone on a schedule that appropriate for that school week. In the end, I actually deferred a lot of the creative direction to the students themselves, and they hardly ran into any problems during the game jam. I feel like this short little game is indicative of my ability to lead others, and teach people with zero knowledge of game development how to make a playable game.
